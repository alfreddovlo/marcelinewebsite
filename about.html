- NEWS FEED ON CHEF MARCELINE -

Marceline Resturant & Bakery was founded by a professional chef called Chef A.D Marceline since 1999 and has always been reputable and famous for its continental meals and the people who dinne here. The resturant and bakery has been recognized all over the world from Europe, Asia, South America and Africa with its amazing meals. The resturant was awarded in Miami (USA) in 2000, 2006, 2014 & 2023 as the best resturant of the year at the "A.B.R.O.Y Awards" and Chef Marceline was picked famously to serve both former President Barack Obama & President Joe Biden of the United State of America.
